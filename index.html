About Me
Engineer. Builder. Problem Solver.

I'm a second-year Electrical Engineering student at Northeastern University with a strong interest in hardware, whether that's power systems, embedded design, or hands-on circuit work. I've always had a passion for problem solving, no matter the scale, and I want to apply that drive throughout my career.
